This Privacy Policy explains how Line Blocks (“the App”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use our Android application.

By installing or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. SUMMARY

Line Blocks is a block puzzle game. Core gameplay works offline on your device. We do not require an account and we do not operate servers to store your game progress.

The App may store your best score and language preference locally on your device. The App shows advertisements through Yandex Mobile Ads SDK and uses AppMetrica for analytics. These third-party services may collect certain technical and usage-related data as described below.

2. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT DIRECTLY

We do not require registration and we do not directly collect:
• your name, email address, or phone number (unless you contact us voluntarily);
• precise GPS location;
• contacts, photos, or other files on your device;
• payment or financial information (the App is free to play).

We do not upload your game board state or full gameplay history to our own servers.

3. INFORMATION PROCESSED ON YOUR DEVICE

The App may store small amounts of data only on your device, for example:
• your best (high) score;
• your selected UI language (if saved);
• standard Android app data needed for the App to function.

This information stays on your device unless described otherwise in this policy (for example, through integrated third-party services).

If Android backup is enabled on your device, some app data may be included in your device backup according to your system settings. We do not control how Google or your device manufacturer handle backups.

4. PERMISSIONS

The App may request or use the following permissions:

• Internet — to load and display advertisements and to send analytics events.
• Access network state — to check connectivity before loading ads or online features.
• Advertising ID (where available and permitted by your device) — used by advertising and analytics SDKs as described below.

We do not request access to your storage, camera, microphone, or contacts for gameplay.

5. ADVERTISING AND THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

5.1 Yandex Mobile Ads

The App uses Yandex Mobile Ads to show:
• a banner advertisement at the bottom of the screen;
• rewarded video advertisements (for example, to continue after game over or unlock bonuses).

Yandex and its partners may collect and process information such as:
• advertising ID (for example, Google Advertising ID), where available and permitted;
• device type, operating system version, language, and general location (for example, country or region);
• IP address;
• app interactions related to ads (for example, impressions, clicks, and rewarded ad completions);
• technical diagnostics to measure ad delivery and prevent fraud.

This data is processed by Yandex and its advertising partners according to their own policies.

Useful links:
• Yandex Advertising Network: https://yandex.com/adv/
• Yandex privacy information: https://yandex.com/legal/confidential/

5.2 AppMetrica Analytics

The App uses Yandex AppMetrica to understand how the App is used and to improve stability and gameplay. AppMetrica may collect information such as:
• device and app identifiers, device model, OS version, and app version;
• language and general region;
• session start and end times and session duration;
• in-app events (for example, game start, game over, restart, bonus use, and interactions with rewarded ads), which may include gameplay-related parameters such as score and game duration.

This data is processed by Yandex according to AppMetrica’s policies.

Useful link:
• AppMetrica: https://appmetrica.yandex.com/

You can limit ad personalization in your device settings (for example: Settings → Google → Ads).

6. LEGAL BASES (EEA/UK USERS)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we rely on:
• contract / legitimate interests — to provide the App, save your local progress, and display ads that support a free app;
• consent — where required for advertising or personalized ads, which you may manage through your device settings.

7. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

The App is a casual puzzle game suitable for a general audience, but it is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

8. DATA RETENTION

• On your device: your best score and preferences remain until you clear app data or uninstall the App.
• Third-party services: retention is governed by Yandex, AppMetrica, and their partners’ policies.

9. DATA SECURITY

We take reasonable measures to protect information within our control. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Because most gameplay data stays on your device, keeping your device secure (screen lock, updates) is important.

10. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

Third-party advertising and analytics services may process data in countries other than your own. By using the App, you understand that such processing may occur according to those providers’ policies and applicable law.

11. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you (if any), object to or restrict certain processing, withdraw consent where processing is consent-based, and lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

To exercise your rights, contact us at games.kkprotasov@gmail.com. For ad- and analytics-related data, you may also use Yandex’s privacy tools and your device ad settings.
